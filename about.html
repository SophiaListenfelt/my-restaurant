How Seaside Came to be

Seaside Coffee was a labor of love created by Sophia and Skyler. Two siblings brought together by a shared passion for coffee and a dream of building something together, they both transformed a cozy, seaside nook into a charming coffee shop. Sophia handled the design and branding, infusing the space with a laid-back, beachy vibe, while her brother took charge of sourcing high-quality beans and perfecting brewing techniques. Together, they crafted a welcoming atmosphere where locals and visitors alike could enjoy expertly brewed coffee and relax by the sea. Seaside Coffee quickly became a beloved spot for everyone in town.
